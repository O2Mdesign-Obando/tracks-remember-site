Trains and Castles

Trains and Castles follows the same journey as The Tracks Remember, but through the eyes of a child.

With movement, curiosity, and imagination at its center, it invites young readers into an adventure shaped by train rides, new places, old stones, big questions, and the excitement of seeing the world unfold.

It is simple, warm, and full of wonder.

A companion to the journey

This book was created as a companion to The Tracks Remember, offering a lighter, child-friendly way to experience the same path.
